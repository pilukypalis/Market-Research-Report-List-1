<p><p>Automotive shunt resistors are precision resistive components used to measure electrical current in vehicles. They operate by allowing a known small amount of current to flow through the resistor, generating a proportional voltage drop that can be measured to determine the total current flow. These resistors are essential in various applications, including battery management systems, electric vehicles, and power distribution systems, where accurate current monitoring is crucial for performance and safety.</p><p>The Automotive Shunt Resistors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.4% during the forecast period.
