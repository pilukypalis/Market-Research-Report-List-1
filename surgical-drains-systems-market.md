<p><strong>The Surgical Drains Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Drains</li><li>Active Drains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical drain systems market comprises two main types: passive drains and active drains. Passive drains rely on gravity and capillary action to remove fluids from the surgical site, commonly featuring simple designs like tubes inserted into the wound. In contrast, active drains incorporate suction mechanisms, such as vacuum devices, to actively remove fluids, allowing for more controlled drainage and reduced risk of complications. Both types are essential for preventing fluid accumulation, promoting healing, and minimizing infection risks post-surgery.</p></p>
