<p><p>The dog nail clippers market includes several types: scissor clippers, guillotine clippers, and grinder tools. Scissor clippers function like traditional scissors, offering precision for thick nails, while guillotine clippers use a blade that shears the nail as it is inserted, providing a clean cut. Grinder tools, often electric or battery-operated, use a rotating sanding surface to gently grind down nails, minimizing the risk of splintering. Each type caters to different preferences and grooming needs, appealing to diverse pet owners.</p></p>
