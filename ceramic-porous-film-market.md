<p><p>The ceramic porous film market is segmented into three main types: plate type, tubular type, and multichannel ceramic films. Plate type ceramic films are flat structures that provide a large surface area for filtration applications. Tubular type ceramic films offer high mechanical strength and are suitable for various separation processes. Multichannel ceramic films consist of several interconnected channels, enhancing filtration efficiency and flow rates. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, including water treatment, chemical processing, and gas filtration.</p></p>
