<p><strong>The Automotive Piezo Buzzer Components Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Piezo Buzzer</li><li>Passive Piezo Buzzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive piezo buzzer components market is categorized into two main types: active and passive piezo buzzers. Active piezo buzzers contain an internal oscillator, allowing them to emit sound with a simple DC voltage supply, making them easy to integrate into electronic systems. In contrast, passive piezo buzzers require an external audio signal or oscillation to produce sound, offering flexibility in tone generation but demanding more complex circuitry. Both types are widely used in vehicle warning systems, alerts, and notifications.</p></p>
