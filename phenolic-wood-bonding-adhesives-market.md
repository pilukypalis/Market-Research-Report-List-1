<p><p>The phenolic wood bonding adhesives market primarily comprises granular and flaky resin types. Granular resin is characterized by its fine particles, allowing for easy mixing and application, often leading to strong and durable bonds in wood products. Flaky resin, on the other hand, features larger flakes that enhance the adhesive's performance by providing better penetration and adhesion to wood surfaces. Both types are widely used in the manufacturing of engineered wood products, furniture, and construction applications, ensuring robust bonding capabilities.</p></p>
