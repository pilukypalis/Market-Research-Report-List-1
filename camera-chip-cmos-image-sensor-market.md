<p><strong>The Camera Chip CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Pixel Passive Sensor (PPS)</li><li>Active Pixel Active Sensor (APS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS image sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Passive Pixel Sensor (PPS) and Active Pixel Sensor (APS). PPS utilizes discontinued pixel technology where each pixel is sensitive but lacks individual read-out circuits, leading to lower image quality and slower capture speeds. In contrast, APS embeds an amplifier within each pixel, allowing for improved image quality, faster processing, and better performance in varying lighting conditions. The APS technology has become dominant due to its superior capabilities and versatility in modern applications.</p></p>
