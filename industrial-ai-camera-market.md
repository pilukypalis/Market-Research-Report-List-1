<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial AI camera market is primarily segmented into two types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture a two-dimensional image of a scene all at once, making them ideal for applications where the entire field of view needs to be analyzed, such as quality control in manufacturing. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, which is beneficial for inspecting longer continuous materials like textiles or packaging, enabling high-speed and high-resolution inspections.</p></p>
