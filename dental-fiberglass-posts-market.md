<p><p>Dental fiberglass posts are used in endodontic treatments to provide support and retention for dental restorations, particularly in cases where the tooth structure has been compromised. Made from a composite of fiberglass and resin materials, these posts offer enhanced aesthetics, biocompatibility, and flexibility compared to traditional metal posts. Their increasing acceptance in the dental community is attributed to their superior properties such as lower risk of root fracture and improved bonding with dentin.</p><p>The Dental Fiberglass Posts Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.9% during the forecast period, driven by rising awareness of dental aesthetics, an increase in the aging population leading to greater dental needs, and advancements in dental technology. Trends in the market indicate a shift towards minimally invasive procedures and the use of high-performance materials, which further enhances the demand for fiberglass posts. Additionally, the growing preference for cosmetic dentistry has fueled the adoption of these posts, as they can be designed to match natural tooth color.
