<p><p>The Magnetic Ink Character Recognition (MICR) printer market primarily consists of two types: laser MICR printers and inkjet MICR printers. Laser MICR printers use toner that incorporates magnetic ink, ensuring high-speed printing and durability, making them suitable for high-volume applications like check processing. In contrast, inkjet MICR printers employ liquid ink with magnetic properties, offering flexibility and lower initial costs, ideal for smaller businesses or lower volume needs. Both types enhance accuracy in capturing and reading MICR codes.</p></p>
