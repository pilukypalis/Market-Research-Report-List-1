<p><strong>The Linear Triblock Copolymer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Anionic Polymerization</li><li>Inactive Anionic Polymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear triblock copolymer market comprises two primary types based on polymerization methods: active and inactive anionic polymerization. Active anionic polymerization involves ongoing reactions where initiators continuously react with monomers, leading to high control over polymer architecture and molecular weight. In contrast, inactive anionic polymerization refers to conditions where the polymerization is halted or limited, resulting in a less controlled process. Both types are critical in producing materials with unique properties suitable for various applications, including adhesives and coatings.</p></p>
