<p><p>The polyester monofilament market comprises three primary types: PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), PBT (Polybutylene Terephthalate), and PEN (Polyethylene Naphthalate). PET is widely used due to its excellent strength and durability, making it ideal for textiles and fishing lines. PBT offers superior thermal and chemical resistance, suitable for engineering applications and automotive parts. PEN, while less common, provides enhanced dimensional stability and is utilized in high-performance applications, particularly in electronics and specialty textiles. Together, these materials cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
