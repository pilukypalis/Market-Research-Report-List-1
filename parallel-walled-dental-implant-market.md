<p><ul><li>Titanium Parallel Walled Dental Implant</li><li>Zirconium Parallel Walled Dental Implant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The parallel-walled dental implant market comprises two main types: titanium and zirconium implants. Titanium parallel-walled implants are widely used due to their strength, biocompatibility, and established long-term performance. They feature a roughened surface that promotes osseointegration. In contrast, zirconium parallel-walled implants are favored for their aesthetic appeal, as they mimic natural tooth color and provide a metal-free alternative. Both types cater to varying patient needs, emphasizing durability and aesthetic considerations in dental restoration.</p></p>
