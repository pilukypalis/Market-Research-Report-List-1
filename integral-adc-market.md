<p><p>The integral ADC market encompasses various types including Charge Balance ADCs and Dual Slope ADCs. Charge Balance ADCs rely on charge redistribution techniques for high precision, making them suitable for low power applications. Dual Slope ADCs integrate an input voltage over time and are known for their accuracy in measurement, often used in digital multimeters. Both types cater to diverse industries, such as automotive and industrial automation, emphasizing precise conversions for various sensor data in electronic systems.</p></p>
