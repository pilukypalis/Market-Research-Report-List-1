<p><strong>The Radioimmunoassays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Balance Method</li><li>Sequential Saturation Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radioimmunoassay market includes two main types: the Balance Method and the Sequential Saturation Method. The Balance Method involves achieving equilibrium between labeled and unlabeled antigens, allowing for quantitative analysis of specific substances. In contrast, the Sequential Saturation Method systematically saturates the binding sites to measure the concentration of antigens in a sample. Both methods leverage radioactively labeled antibodies to enhance sensitivity and specificity, catering to diverse applications in clinical diagnostics and research settings.</p></p>
