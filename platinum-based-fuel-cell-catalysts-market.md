<p><strong>The Platinum-based Fuel Cell Catalysts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pt/C</li><li>Pt Alloy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platinum-based fuel cell catalysts primarily include Pt/C and Pt Alloy types. Pt/C catalysts consist of platinum nanoparticles dispersed on carbon supports, offering high activity for reactions like hydrogen oxidation and oxygen reduction. They are widely used due to their efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Pt Alloy catalysts, on the other hand, combine platinum with other metals to enhance activity and reduce platinum usage, improving overall performance and durability. Both types are crucial for advancing fuel cell technology in energy applications.</p></p>
