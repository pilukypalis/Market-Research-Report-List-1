<p><strong>The Cardiopulmonary Bypass Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Roller Pump</li><li>Double Roller Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiopulmonary bypass pumps market includes two main types: single roller pumps and double roller pumps. Single roller pumps utilize one roller mechanism to propel blood, providing simplicity and ease of management during surgery. In contrast, double roller pumps employ two rollers, which enhance blood flow stability and reduce shear stress, minimizing potential damage to blood cells. Both types are crucial for maintaining circulation and oxygenation during cardiac procedures, with their choice depending on the specific clinical requirements and preferences of surgical teams.</p></p>
