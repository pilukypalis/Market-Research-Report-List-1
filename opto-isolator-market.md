<p><ul><li>Non-linear Opto-isolator</li><li>Linear Opto-isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The opto-isolator market is divided into two main types: linear and non-linear opto-isolators. Linear opto-isolators are designed for applications requiring precise signal transmission and high linearity, making them suitable for analog signals in control systems and audio electronics. In contrast, non-linear opto-isolators are used primarily in digital signal applications, providing isolation while allowing for faster switching speeds. Both types play critical roles in protecting circuits from noise and electrical surges across various industries.</p></p>
