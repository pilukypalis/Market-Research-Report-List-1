<p><p>Facial coding in marketing is categorized into two main types: Single Facial Coding and Multiplayer Facial Coding. Single Facial Coding involves analyzing the emotional responses of an individual to a specific stimulus, such as an advertisement or product, capturing their reactions to gauge effectiveness. In contrast, Multiplayer Facial Coding measures the emotional responses of multiple individuals simultaneously, often in focus groups or social settings, allowing brands to assess collective sentiments and interactions, providing deeper insights into group dynamics and shared reactions.</p></p>
