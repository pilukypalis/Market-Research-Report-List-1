<p><ul><li>Differential U-Tube Manometer</li><li>Inverted U-Tube Manometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The U-Tube Manometer market consists of two primary types: Differential U-Tube Manometers and Inverted U-Tube Manometers. Differential U-Tube Manometers measure the pressure difference between two points, making them ideal for various industrial applications. Inverted U-Tube Manometers, on the other hand, are used to measure the pressure of gases or liquids, relying on the height difference of fluid columns. Both types are essential for accurate pressure measurement in laboratory, HVAC, and fluid dynamics applications, driving demand in multiple sectors.</p></p>
