<p><ul><li>Drip Irrigation</li><li>Micro/Mini Sprinkler Irrigation</li><li>Conventional Sprinkler Irrigation</li><li>Traditional Sprinklers</li><li>Central Pivot Sprinklers</li><li>Lateral Move Sprinklers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro irrigation system market encompasses several types designed for efficient water use. Drip irrigation delivers water directly to plant roots, minimizing evaporation. Micro and mini sprinklers provide localized coverage, suitable for smaller areas. Conventional sprinkler systems distribute water over larger fields, while traditional sprinklers use fixed or rotating nozzles. Central pivot sprinklers rely on a rotating arm to irrigate large fields, and lateral move sprinklers traverse fields, ensuring uniform watering. Each method optimizes water efficiency for diverse agricultural needs.</p></p>
