<p><p>The sperm separation systems market comprises three main types: on-chip, electrophoresis, and dielectrophoresis. On-chip systems utilize microfluidic technologies to isolate sperm efficiently within a compact platform. Electrophoresis leverages electric fields to separate sperm based on size and charge, enhancing purity for assisted reproductive techniques. Dielectrophoresis employs non-uniform electric fields to manipulate sperm cells based on their dielectric properties, allowing for selective separation. Together, these technologies play a vital role in improving fertility treatments and reproductive health outcomes.</p></p>
