<p><p>The neurological monitoring device market is categorized into non-invasive and invasive types. Invasive devices involve surgical procedures to directly access neural structures, offering precise data for critical conditions but with higher risks. Both types are essential for diagnosing and managing neurological disorders, with non-invasive methods preferred for routine assessments and invasive methods reserved for complex cases requiring detailed monitoring.</p></p>
