<p><ul><li>Peel Strength</li><li>Tack Strength</li><li>Shear Strength</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adhesives testing market includes various strength assessments to evaluate adhesive performance. Peel strength measures the force required to separate adhesive bonds, indicating durability in applications like labels and tapes. Tack strength assesses the stickiness of an adhesive upon initial contact, crucial for products needing immediate bonding. Shear strength evaluates the adhesive's resistance to sliding forces, essential for structural integrity in industrial applications. Together, these tests ensure that adhesives meet specific performance standards essential for diverse applications.</p></p>
