Single Turbo systems boost engine performance by using one turbocharger, ideal for smaller engines. Twin Turbo setups utilize two turbos for enhanced efficiency and power, often seen in high-performance applications. Electro-Assist Turbo systems incorporate electric motors to support turbocharging, providing instant power and improving response times. Each type caters to specific performance needs, optimizing fuel efficiency and engine output across various applications.</p></p>
