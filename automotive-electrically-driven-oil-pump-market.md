<p><p>The automotive electrically driven oil pump market is primarily categorized into two types: integrated pumps and separate pumps. Integrated pumps are designed as a part of the vehicle's engine system, optimizing space and efficiency by combining oil pumping functionality with other components. In contrast, separate pumps are standalone units that can be independently installed and operated, offering flexibility in applications and maintenance. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing engine performance and improving fuel efficiency in modern vehicles.</p></p>
