<p><p>The Closed Caption Services market comprises two main types: Real-Time Captioning and Prerecorded Captioning. Real-Time Captioning provides immediate textual translation of spoken content during live events, making it essential for broadcasts, conferences, and meetings. It enhances accessibility for viewers who are deaf or hard of hearing. Prerecorded Captioning, on the other hand, involves adding captions to previously recorded content, such as films and videos, ensuring that viewers can follow along at their convenience while maintaining engagement and clarity.</p></p>
