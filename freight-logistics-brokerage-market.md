<p><p>Freight logistics brokerage operates primarily in three market types: truckload, less-than-truckload (LTL), and other markets. Truckload involves transporting large shipments exclusively using an entire truck, making it cost-effective for bulk shipments. LTL combines shipments from multiple customers, allowing for smaller loads and reducing costs for individual shippers. Other markets include specialized services such as intermodal transportation, air freight, and oversized freight, catering to unique logistics needs beyond standard truckload and LTL options.</p></p>
