<p><p>The electrophysiology lab system market encompasses various specialized technologies designed for cardiac care. X-ray systems aid in visualizing heart structures during procedures. 3D mapping systems provide detailed spatial representations of electrical activity in the heart. EP recording systems capture and analyze heart signals. Remote steering systems enable precise catheter manipulation from a distance. Intracardiac echocardiography systems offer real-time imaging within the heart. Radiofrequency (RF) ablation generators deliver targeted energy to disrupt abnormal electrical pathways, facilitating effective treatment of arrhythmias.</p></p>
