<p><strong>The 5G Indoor Small Cells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sub-6GHz</li><li>mmWAVE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G indoor small cells market comprises two main types: Sub-6GHz and mmWave. Sub-6GHz small cells provide extended coverage and are ideal for broader reach and penetration through walls, making them suitable for larger indoor environments. In contrast, mmWave small cells offer higher data rates and capacity but have limited range and penetration capabilities, making them best suited for high-density areas like stadiums or urban centers. Together, these technologies enhance indoor coverage and user experience.</p></p>
