<p><p>The Non-Invasive Positive Pressure Ventilation Devices market comprises mainly CPAP and BiPAP devices. CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) devices deliver a steady airflow to keep airways open, primarily used for sleep apnea treatment. BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) devices provide two pressure levels, one for inhalation and another for exhalation, making them suitable for patients with more complex respiratory conditions. Both devices enhance patient comfort and improve ventilation without requiring invasive procedures, catering to diverse healthcare needs.</p></p>
