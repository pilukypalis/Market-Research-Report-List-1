<p><ul><li>Resting & Stress ECG Systems</li><li>Holter Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diagnostic ECG market comprises three main types: Resting ECG systems, Stress ECG systems, and Holter monitors. Resting ECG systems capture the heart's electrical activity at rest, providing baseline cardiac information. Stress ECG systems evaluate heart performance during physical exertion, identifying potential issues under stress. Holter monitors are portable devices worn over 24 to 48 hours, offering continuous heart rate and rhythm tracking to detect irregularities over time. Together, these technologies enhance cardiac diagnosis and monitoring.</p></p>
