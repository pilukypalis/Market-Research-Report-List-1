Injection molding machinery is used for producing complex shapes by injecting molten plastic into molds. Extruders machinery shapes plastic by forcing it through a die, ideal for continuous production. Blow molding machinery creates hollow plastic products by inflating heated plastic in a mold. Together, these technologies cater to diverse applications in packaging, automotive, consumer goods, and more.</p></p>
