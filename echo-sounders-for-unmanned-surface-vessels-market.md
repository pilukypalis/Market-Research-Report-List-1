<p><strong>The Echo Sounders for Unmanned Surface Vessels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Beam Echo Sounders</li><li>Multi-beam Echo Sounders</li><li>Side-Scan Sonar Echo Sounders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Echo sounders for unmanned surface vessels (USVs) are essential for underwater mapping and surveying. Single-beam echo sounders provide depth measurements along a single line, offering basic information about the seafloor. Multi-beam echo sounders utilize multiple beams to create detailed, high-resolution maps, capturing large areas efficiently. Side-scan sonar echo sounders, on the other hand, are designed to produce detailed images of the seafloor and submerged objects, making them ideal for features such as wreck detection and habitat mapping.</p></p>
