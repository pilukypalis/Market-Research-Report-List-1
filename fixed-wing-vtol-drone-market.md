<p><p>The fixed-wing VTOL drone market can be categorized into three main types based on propulsion systems: electric, gasoline, and hybrid. Electric drones are powered by batteries, offering low noise and maintenance costs but limited flight time. Gasoline drones utilize internal combustion engines, providing longer ranges and heavier payload capacities, making them suitable for commercial applications. Hybrid drones combine both systems, leveraging the advantages of electric power for vertical takeoff and transition to gasoline for extended flight endurance, thus enhancing versatility and efficiency.</p></p>
