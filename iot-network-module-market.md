<p><p>The IoT network module market comprises various technologies catering to distinct connectivity needs. LoRa (Long Range) is ideal for low-power, wide-area applications, enabling long-distance communication. NB-IoT (Narrowband IoT) focuses on cellular connectivity for devices in remote locations with minimal power usage. LTE-M (Long Term Evolution for Machines) supports higher data rates and mobility for IoT devices. Wi-Fi is suitable for local, high-speed connections. Other emerging technologies include Zigbee and Sigfox, catering to specific application requirements in the IoT ecosystem.</p></p>
