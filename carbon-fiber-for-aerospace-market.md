<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber for Aerospace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Regular-Tow Carbon Fiber</li><li>Large-Tow Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the aerospace market, carbon fiber is categorized into two primary types: regular-tow carbon fiber and large-tow carbon fiber. Regular-tow carbon fiber features finer strands, typically used in applications requiring high performance and strength-to-weight ratios, making it ideal for critical aerospace components. In contrast, large-tow carbon fiber consists of thicker strands, allowing for cost-effective manufacturing while still offering substantial mechanical properties. This type is often utilized in less demanding applications where weight savings are crucial but extreme performance is not essential.</p></p>
