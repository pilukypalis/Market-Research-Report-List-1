<p><p>The abdominal ultrasound probe market encompasses various types, primarily ceramic probes and single crystal probes. Ceramic probes utilize piezoelectric ceramics for efficient sound wave generation and are known for durability and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, single crystal probes offer superior image resolution and sensitivity, as they use a single piece of crystal to produce ultrasound waves. This distinction allows healthcare providers to choose between a more economical option or a high-performance probe based on their diagnostic needs and budget constraints.</p></p>
